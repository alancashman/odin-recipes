Lasagna

Description

Cheese and tomato sauce combine their powers to create the pasta equivalent of cake. Melty, cheesy, gooey, goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar Tomato Sauce
  • 1 box Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 container Ricotta Cheese
  • 2 cups shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 bunch Spinach

Steps

  1. Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch).
  2. Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce.
  3. Top with remaining 3 uncooked noodles and remaining pasta sauce. Cover.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour.
  5. Uncover and top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Let stand 5 minutes.
